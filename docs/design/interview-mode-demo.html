Readability notes

  • 16px body at 1.62 line-height, weight 300 — matches the existing SystemAudioView transcript. Thin weight feels editorial for long reading; the 1.62 line-height gives room for eye tracking across the 780px max content width (optimal 50–75 CPL).
  • Speaker names and accents carry two channels — colour + dot glyph. If someone's printing the transcript in greyscale or has reduced colour perception, the dot still lands.
  • Timestamps in mono tabular figures — 14:30:12 in a monospace font means the column stays rigid, scannable, and doesn't wiggle as seconds tick up in the live feed.
  • Uppercase labels with 0.2em tracking — matches the note page's "VAULT EXPLORER" / "SIDE NOTES" rhythm. Consistency across pages signals "this is the same app."
  • Atmosphere stays behind a 12px backdrop blur — text never sits directly on a shifting gradient, so contrast stays stable during blob drift.
