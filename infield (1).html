Q3 Retro — Decisions

#retro #helix #planning + tag

Three decisions came out of the Q3 retro. Each is owned by a single person and has an explicit date we'll revisit it. No other changes until then — we over-changed last quarter and paid for it.

The decisions

  1. Schema freeze · 6 weeks. No migrations until 2026-05-31. PPriya
  2. Release gate behind canary.pct ≥ 25. Promotion requires sustained telemetry at 25% share, minimum 36 hour soak. TTheo
  3. Two sprints on customer latency. Platform team redirects. P95 target: < 180 ms at the edge. MMaya

What we’d do differently

The bleed between telemetry and release cadence cost us visibility for roughly nine days at the worst moment of the rollout. Going forward we commit to not shipping a schema change and a feature flag in the same week — see the linked runbook in [[Helix runbook §4]].
