Board & Grid card — what shows on a card?

What fields appear on a card in Board and Grid views

A

Title + up to 2 secondary fields

Card shows: primary field (title) prominently, then up to 2 key secondary fields as small colored chips or muted text lines. Consistent with Notion.

B

Title + all non-empty fields as chips

Card shows the title, then ALL other populated fields as small colored chips. More information density, noisier.

C

Title + cover image + 1 subtitle

Card has an optional cover image area at top (from a URL field or gradient), title below, then one subtitle field. More visual, better for image-heavy databases.
