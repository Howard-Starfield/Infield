Board card design — AppFlowy-style rich cards

Based on AppFlowy: cards support checkbox + text + colored labels in any combination

A

AppFlowy-style — inline block cards

Cards are rich content blocks: checkbox inline, text lines, colored label chips. The "+" button inside the column adds a card. Another "+" on the right edge of the column header adds a new column.

B

Classic kanban — title + field chips only

Cards show a single title field + up to 2 secondary field chips. Simpler, less flexible. Board groups by a Select field.
