AddViewPopup — the "+" button behavior

When the user clicks [+] on the right side of the tab bar

A

Popover below [+] button

A small dropdown/popover appears directly below the [+] button with the list of view types. Clicking outside dismisses it. Instant, unobtrusive.

B

Modal dialog (centered)

A centered modal with a larger preview of each view type and descriptions. More ceremonial — good if views are unfamiliar to the user.

C

Slide-out panel from right

The panel slides in from the right edge of the tab bar, showing view types with mini-previews. Slightly more spacious, stays out of center screen.
