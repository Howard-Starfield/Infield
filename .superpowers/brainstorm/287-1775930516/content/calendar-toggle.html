A

Segmented Control

"Month" | "Week" pill buttons in the toolbar. Compact, native OS feel, sits between title and nav arrows. My recommendation.

B

Tab Bar

Full-width underline tabs: Month / Week / Year. Clear hierarchy, easy to discover. But takes vertical space.

C

Icon Toggle

Small icon buttons far right of the toolbar. Most compact. Best when toolbar space is tight.
