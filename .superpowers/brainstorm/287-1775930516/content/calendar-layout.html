A

Month Grid

Classic monthly calendar. Drag events between day cells to reschedule. Dense — shows 35 days at once. Best for overview planning.

B

Week + Agenda

7-day week strip with time slots. Events sized by duration. Drag vertically to change time, horizontally to change day. Best for time-blocked work.

C

Both (toggle)

Tab or toggle between Month view and Week view. Both support drag-to-reschedule. Most flexible — but more UI surface to build and maintain.
