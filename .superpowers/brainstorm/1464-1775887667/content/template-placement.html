C — New board creation flow

Templates only surface when creating a new board. Save lives in the ⋯ menu; load appears in the "create board" modal. Templates feel intentional — not cluttering everyday use.

You can also mix these — e.g. B (save in ⋯ menu) + C (load when creating). Let me know in the terminal if a combo feels right.
